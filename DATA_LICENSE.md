# Data licensing

The Apache-2.0 software license does not automatically license catalogue data,
prices, source documents, retailer exports, product images, or trademarks.

## Public project-originated data

The following project-originated files are available under
[CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/):

- `shared/specs/smartphones.v1.json`
- `shared/specs/fixtures/`

The fixtures are synthetic contract examples, not claims about real products.
Attribution may be provided as: `qiymetleri.com contributors, CC BY 4.0`.

## Excluded data

No licence is granted for:

- production catalogue records and price history;
- real pilot snapshots or database exports;
- raw or normalized retailer/manufacturer source documents;
- source-specific one-off ingestion payloads;
- product or retailer images and logos;
- private deployment, analytics, moderation, or operational data.

Those materials live under the ignored `private/` overlay or external managed
storage. A public fact is not automatically redistributable as a compiled
database; verify source terms and applicable database rights before publishing
any derived dataset.
